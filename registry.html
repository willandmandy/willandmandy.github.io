Gifts

Cash?

Why yes, we will gladly accept your hard-earned fiat currency. We quite enjoy the feeling of depositing a paper cheque in the bank, but feel free to send an Interac® or Zelle® to Will or Amanda if preferred. CAD and USD are equally acceptable.

Objects?

Materialism isn't really our jam, so we'd much prefer paper or digital objects that can be exchanged for, say, a honeymoon to Ireland. I believe the term the kids are using these days is "money."
But sentimental or meaningful objects are fine too, if that's more to your taste. Please, no kitchenware.

No Gift At All?

I mean... a Spotify "mixed tape" would be nice, at least, but sure, we'd rather that you came with nothing than that you didn't come at all!
