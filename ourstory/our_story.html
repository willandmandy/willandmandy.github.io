Our Story

When We Met

We met at that most romantic of venues: a bar.
The Ship and Anchor to be precise.
We were soon inseparable, celebrating holidays together
and enjoying the mountains every chance we had,
taking in the incredible sights at our doorstep.

When We Grew

Things progressed quickly,
and we slowed down a bit.
Luckily, Elliot liked getting out of the house too, joining us on our excursions.
And he didn't mind too much when we took a little "us" time in a vineyard.
We shared time with families near
and far,
in climates tropical
and nigh-arctic,
in locked rooms
and lockdowns.
And above all, we kept it classy.

When We Grew Again

And then it happened again: our little family grew once more.
Adriana was a delight. And she, like her brother, took quickly to the rocky crags
(and her mother's peculiar obsession with A. A. Milne).
Though things are sometimes moving fast
We still take time to slow things down
And celebrate our love for each other.
So please join us, if you can, to celebrate together!
